Oct 2017 - Apr 2019

INATEL, Brazil
Research Intern (Flying Ad Hoc Networks)

  • Worked on a research project, “Fault-Tolerant Moving Resource Mutual Exclusion Algorithm for FANET” under the guidance of Dr. Joel J. P. C. Rodrigues at National Institute of Telecommunications, Brazil.

  • Devised a novel algorithm to resolve classical resource allocation problem (Mutual Exclusion) in distributed Flying Ad Hoc Networks. System model: multiple nodes requesting to access shared resources placed on flying UAVs are allocated in a mutually exclusive way, was architected using THE ONE framework. Algorithm was made fault tolerant and performance studies concluded that algorithm exhibits scalable performance under heavy contention (load).
